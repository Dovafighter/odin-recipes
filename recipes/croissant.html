Croissant French Toast

Description

This toast is half American, half French.

Ingredients

  • 4 large croissants
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 95 degress (C)
    2. Whisk eggs and dip croissant into them
    3. Mix
    4. Serve warm with toppings
