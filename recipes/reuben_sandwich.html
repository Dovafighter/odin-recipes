Reuben Sandwich

Description

This sandwich will not leave you hungry.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices rye bread
  • Thousand Island dressing
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese

    • Steps

    1. Preheat skillet or a large giddle
    2. Make sandwiches with the ingredients
    3. Place them, butter-side down, on the preheated griddle
    4. Serve hot
