The World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is time consuming but definitely worth the effort. Just like every good thing in life.

Ingredients

  • Meat, a lot of meat
  • Onion and two gloves of garlic
  • Can of tomatoes
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices. Some salit, pepeer, you know
  • Noodles. A lot of noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cooke the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest and enjoy the meal
